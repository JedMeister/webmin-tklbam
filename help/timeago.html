Time to Restore From

Timestamps for restorable backup sessions can be found in the Hub's backup section.

TIME := YYYY-MM-DD | YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss | <int>[mhDWMY]

  e.g.,

    2010-08-06           2010, August 6th, 00:00 
    2010-08-07T14:00     2010, August 7th 14:00 UTC 
    6m                   6 minutes 
    5h                   5 hours 
    4D                   4 days ago 
    3W                   3 weeks ago 
    2M                   2 months ago 
    1Y                   1 year ago
